# Constitution — Part 1: Vision & Mission (ClaimPilot)

**Vision:** Stop independent practices from writing off revenue they earned. Every denied claim deserves a fighting chance.

**Mission:** Give independent medical, dental, and specialty practices an AI that ingests denials (EOBs/835s), diagnoses root cause, drafts payer-specific appeals with the right clinical + policy citations, and tracks every appeal to resolution — recovering cash that would otherwise be lost.

**Core principles:**
1. **HIPAA-first** — PHI safety, BAA-covered infra, minimization/de-identification by default; compliance is non-negotiable and a moat.
2. **Grounded appeals** — every appeal cites real payer rules + clinical/policy basis; no fabricated medical or coverage claims.
3. **ROI is the product** — measure and prove recovered cash; low churn follows undeniable ROI.
4. **Human-in-the-loop** — clinicians/billers approve appeals before submission; agent assists, never auto-submits sensitive content unreviewed.
5. **Compounding knowledge** — payer-rules + denial-reason knowledge base deepens per specialty/payer.
6. **Lean & cost-disciplined** — serverless/managed BAA infra, RAG over giant context, cheap models for classification.

**What we are not:** a hospital-scale enterprise RCM suite; an auto-submitter of unreviewed clinical claims; a generic chatbot.

**North-star:** recovered claim revenue per practice per month + appeal win rate.

**Consistency check:** sets HIPAA bar (Part 7/8), grounding (Part 6), ROI metrics (Part 12), reseller GTM (Part 11).
