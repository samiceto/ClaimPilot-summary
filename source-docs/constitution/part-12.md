# Constitution — Part 12: Success Metrics (ClaimPilot)

**North-star:** **recovered claim revenue per practice per month** + **appeal win rate**.

**Customer outcome KPIs:**
- $ recovered per practice; recovery rate (appeals won / filed).
- Denial write-off reduction; time-to-first-appeal; deadline adherence (target 100% — no missed appeals).
- Staff time saved per appeal.

**Product KPIs:**
- Activation (ingest + first appeal drafted) > 55%.
- Appeal approval/edit rate (% AI drafts approved with minor edits).
- AI quality: denial-classification accuracy > 90%; extraction accuracy high; citation correctness; hallucination ≈0 on clinical/coverage; success-likelihood calibration.
- Payer-rules KB coverage growth per specialty/payer.

**Business KPIs:**
- MRR/ARR; blended ACV ~$6,000; NRR > 115%; logo retention (target very low churn).
- Gross margin > 82%; AI COGS < 10% revenue.
- CAC payback < 7 mo; reseller-sourced pipeline %.

**Compliance/reliability KPIs:** 0 PHI-leak incidents; 100% PHI access audited; 0 missed deadlines; 99.9% uptime.

**Targets:** MRR $25–65k within first cohorts (per summary); expansion via providers + performance fee + resellers.

**Review cadence:** weekly product/AI-quality + compliance; monthly business + cohort retention + recovered-revenue reporting.

**Consistency check:** measures vision/ROI (Part 1), grounding (Part 6), HIPAA (Part 7), business model (Part 3).
