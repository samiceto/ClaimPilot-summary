# Project 4 — ClaimPilot

**Category:** Healthcare RCM · Vertical AI SaaS · B2B

## Project Summary

- **Name:** ClaimPilot
- **Elevator Pitch:** ClaimPilot is the AI that **wins back denied insurance claims** for independent medical and dental practices. It ingests denials (EOBs/835s), diagnoses the root cause, drafts a payer-specific appeal letter with the right clinical and policy citations, and tracks every appeal to resolution — recovering revenue that small practices today simply write off.
- **Problem Solved:** Claim denials silently erase a large share of independent-practice revenue; staff lack time/expertise to appeal, and enterprise RCM platforms (Waystar-class) target hospitals, not small practices.
- **Target Customer:** Practice managers, billers, and owner-clinicians at independent medical, dental, and specialty practices (1–25 providers) and small billing companies.
- **Target Industry:** Outpatient healthcare, dental, behavioral health, physical therapy, small RCM/billing firms.
- **Revenue Model:** Subscription per provider/location — Solo $149/mo, Practice $449/mo, Group $1,200/mo — plus optional performance fee on recovered claims; billing-company reseller tier.
- **Competitive Advantage:** A payer-rules + denial-reason knowledge base that compounds per specialty/payer, plus HIPAA-grade infrastructure that is itself a barrier to entry. ROI is undeniable (recovered cash), driving very low churn.
- **Market Score:** 8 / 10
- **Competition Score:** 8 / 10 (enterprise-focused incumbents; independent-practice layer under-served)
- **Estimated MVP Time:** 10–12 weeks (HIPAA + integrations)
- **Estimated MVP Cost:** $6,000–$10,000 out-of-pocket (BAA-covered infra, compliance)
- **Estimated Monthly Cost (at ~50 customers):** $1,000–$1,800 (HIPAA infra, PHI handling)
- **Estimated Monthly Revenue Potential (12-mo target):** $25k–$65k MRR

## Assumptions
- HIPAA/BAA from day one; PHI minimization and de-identification where possible.
- Longer sales cycle offset by very low churn + high ROI + reseller (billing-company) leverage.
- Positioned as the portfolio's defensibility / long-term-moat play, not fastest-to-profit.
